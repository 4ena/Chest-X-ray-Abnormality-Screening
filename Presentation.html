WHAT WE LEARNED

Growth in 24 Hours

Medical Imaging Data

First time working with DICOM-style datasets and understanding CheXpert's uncertainty labels (blanks, ones, zeros, U-labels).

PyTorch Augmentation

Built custom transform pipelines — rotation, elastic deformation, random erasing — to prevent overfitting on limited data.

AUC Optimization

Discovered DeepAUC and learned why optimizing AUC directly (instead of cross-entropy) matters for imbalanced medical datasets.
